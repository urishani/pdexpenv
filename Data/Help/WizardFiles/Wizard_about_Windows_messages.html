The Window located at the top right of the screen is the Messages Window. This is where the system displays messages or errors after executing a wizard.

You can scroll back to previous messages with the scroll bar or clear the messages from the window with the "Clear Messages" button located at the top of the window.

Messages are printed in blue. Errors are printed in red. More information on errors can be found on the tools page.
