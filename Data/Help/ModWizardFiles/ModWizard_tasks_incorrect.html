* Incorrect results
If you executed a progam and did not recieve the results required by the task, you will see a pop up message telling you that you have Incorrect reuslts.
 
You will also see the penalty (in points) that will be removed from you total score will be displayed in the pop up message window.
 
You may have one of three types of errors:
      * You selected the correct cells in the table, but the value in the cells
        are not what they are supposed to be.
        
      * You selected the incorrect cells in the table, but the values of the cells you selected
        are what you are required by the task description.
 
      * You did not select the correct cells in the table AND the values of the cells you
        selected are not what is required by the task.
 
After you click the OK button on the pop up message window, you will be given a new task to complete.
