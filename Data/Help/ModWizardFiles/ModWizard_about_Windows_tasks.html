The Window located at the top left of the screen is the Task Window. Each task you have to do is displayed in this window.
The top part displays the instructions telling you to choose a wizard from the 9 wizards at the bottom of the task window.

The middle part displays the description of the task you have to do. After completing a task the next task is displayed here too.

The bottom part displays 9 buttons - each activating one of the 9 different wizard tools.
