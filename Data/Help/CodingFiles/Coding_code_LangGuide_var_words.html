There are certain words that you cannot use as variables. These are words that are part of the language and are RESERVED for its use only.
 
If you try to create a variable that is named with one of the reserved words you will receive a compilation error.
 
The following words are reserved by the language and CANNOT be used as names for variables:
 
AND, do, for, endif, else, elseif, if, loop, next, OR, print, REM, table.
